Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about campus tours, scheduling, and more.

General Questions

How can I apply for a campus tour?

To apply for a campus tour, log in to the 'Highschool Application Tracking System' on your dashboard, select a date and time, and follow the on-screen instructions.

How much does it cost to schedule a campus tour?

All campus tours organized through Bilkent University Information Office are completely free of charge.

How far in advance should I schedule my campus tour?

We recommend scheduling your tour at least 2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred time slot.

Account and Access

How do I create an account?

Visit the login page and click 'Register'. Fill out the required information and verify your email address to activate your account.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

If you forgot your password, click the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

How do I update my account details?

After logging in, navigate to 'Settings' in your dashboard. From there, you can update your personal details, change your password, and manage preferences.

Tour Information

How long does a campus tour take?

A standard campus tour takes approximately 1.5 to 2 hours. Additional time may be needed for special sessions or Q&A.

What should I bring with me on the campus tour?

We recommend bringing a valid ID, comfortable walking shoes, and any questions you might have about the university or programs.

Can I bring a group to the campus tour?

Yes, group tours can be arranged. Please mention the group size and contact details when filling out the application form.

Technical Issues

Why am I unable to log in to my account?

Ensure that you are entering the correct email and password. If the issue persists, try resetting your password or contact support at agorateam@bilkent.edu.tr.

What should I do if I encounter a technical error?

If you experience any technical issues, please report them to our technical team by emailing agorateam@bilkent.edu.tr.
